I adore graphic visualizations, and if I can add some rich interactivity to the mix, I am one happy camper. I experimented with this exact idea for the launch of Salva O'Renick's new website and a new stance of being very transparent in their web presence. By showing visitors the trends of traffic on the very website they are viewing, they gain an understanding of how the site is used and how close they are to a common user, and in turn, hopefully they will grasp how this all relates to the user experience of the site. The Flash examples I have on this page are a random sampling of used and non-used visualizations I was playing with that pulled in Google Analytic Data.
