Central Bancompany's "In Your Pocket" commercial aired across Missouri and Kansas in Summer 2010, launching the bank's mobile website. As technical director and post-production artist for the video, I was able to see the initial concept brought to life, from start to finish. With the help of Liquid 9's production prowess and the sweet illustrations of my co-worker Matt Shaw, we were able to encompass the idea of "banking that knows no distance" in a very fun and uplifting way. The making of the video by my friend Dave Gilbertson in the menu to the left gives some insight on how this gem was crafted.
