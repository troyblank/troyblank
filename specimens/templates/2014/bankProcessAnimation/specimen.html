Bank Process Animation


Javascript | ID 017 | 2014

People Have sort attention spans, and when viewing web pages there are no exceptions. Unfortunetly when verify a user with a bank Dwolla found out that this process can range from super fast to slugish depending on the bank. In order to keep the waiter engaged we needed a way to entertain and show progress at the same time. This SVG animation does just the trick; built super light weight and responsive using vector graphics this little slice of cool gives a user something to gawk at for the time their bank deems necesary to verify their account.
